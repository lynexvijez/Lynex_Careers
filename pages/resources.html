Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about applying to Lynex

How long does the hiring process take?

Typically 2-4 weeks from application to offer, depending on the role and interview availability.

Do you offer remote positions?

Yes, many of our roles are available for remote work. We're a distributed-first company.

What's your approach to diversity and inclusion?

We're committed to building diverse teams and have specific programs to ensure equitable hiring.

Can I apply for multiple positions?

Yes, but we recommend focusing on roles that best match your skills and interests.

Do you provide visa sponsorship?

Yes, we sponsor visas for qualified candidates in most of our locations.

What's the career growth potential at Lynex?

We have clear growth paths and promote from within whenever possible.
